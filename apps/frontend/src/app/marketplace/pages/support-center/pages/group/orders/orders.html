Modifying Your Order

Need to make changes to your order? Here's what you can do:

Before Shipment

You can modify delivery address, add items, or cancel your order. Contact support immediately for assistance.

After Shipment

Once shipped, orders cannot be modified. You may refuse delivery or initiate a return after receiving the items.

Common Order Questions

How long does it take to receive my order?

Standard delivery takes 2-5 business days within major cities. Remote areas may take 5-7 business days. Express delivery (1-2 days) is available at checkout.

Can I track my order?

Yes! Once shipped, you'll receive a tracking number via email and SMS. Use it to monitor your delivery in real-time on our website or the courier's platform.

What if I'm not home for delivery?

Our courier will attempt delivery up to 3 times. You can also arrange to pick up from the nearest courier depot or reschedule delivery through the tracking portal.

Can someone else receive my order?

Yes, anyone at the delivery address can receive your order. For high-value items, we may require ID verification.

What are the delivery charges?

Delivery charges vary by location and order size. Small items start from KES 200. Free delivery on orders over KES 5,000 within major cities.
