Payment FAQs

When is my payment processed?

Payments are processed immediately upon order confirmation. You'll receive a payment receipt via email and SMS.

What if my payment fails?

If payment fails, check your payment details and try again. Contact your bank or payment provider if issues persist. Your order will be held for 24 hours.

Can I change my payment method after ordering?

Unfortunately, payment methods cannot be changed after order placement. You may cancel the order and place a new one with your preferred payment method.

Do you offer payment plans?

For bulk orders and wholesale purchases, we offer flexible payment terms. Contact our sales team for more information.
